Rays Mountain Bike Park is the worlds first indoor mountain bike park. Built in 2004 the indoor park has been adding features left and right to meet the needs and wants of the community. The park has many sections involving a technical section, pump track, jump section, and a 2 tier path going all the way around the park.

Welcome

Way back on a cold, wet and nasty Thanksgiving weekend in 1996 I had a crazy dream-“Let’s get an old factory building and build an indoor mountain bike paradise!” That dream came to life Thanksgiving weekend 2004. It’s hard to believe but it’s the start of our 14th season. It’s been a long, hard and fun ride. Through the years we’ve tried to build a fun experience for every level of rider with a fun family atmosphere. I hope you will come and check us out. We’re always working hard making a better park for you! Hope to see you at the park! Thanks for being a part of the dream.

- Ray
